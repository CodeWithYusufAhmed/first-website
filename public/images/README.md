# /public/images

Drop your four hero photos here with these exact filenames:

| Filename       | What it is                                             | Suggested aspect |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | ---------------- |
| `boat.jpg`     | Portrait on a wooden boat, blue sky behind (hero image) | ~4:5 portrait    |
| `jaago.jpg`    | Volunteering at JAAGO Foundation, holding the kid      | ~3:2             |
| `guitar.jpg`   | Playing guitar at night on the rooftop                 | ~3:2             |
| `cycling.jpg`  | On the bike, red shirt, BDCyclists era                 | ~3:2             |

Optional but recommended:
- `og-cover.jpg` — 1200×630 social share card. Put your favorite framed shot here.
- `apple-touch-icon.png` — 180×180 PNG for iOS home-screen icon.

JPG, PNG, or WebP all work. Keep each under ~300 KB after compression to keep
Lighthouse Performance happy. [Squoosh.app](https://squoosh.app) is a quick
in-browser way to crunch them.
